System Requirements :::
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* RAM: A absolute minimum of 2 GB is required for the OS, but 4 GB or more is highly recommended for running multiple containers.
* Disk Space: At least 10 GiB of available disk space for the base installation; however, 20–30 GB is recommended to accommodate container images and local storage.
* CPU/Architecture: Supports 64-bit architectures, specifically:x86_64 (AMD and Intel, requiring x86-64-v2 microarchitecture).ARM64 (aarch64, ARMv8.0-A).IBM Z (z14) and IBM Power Systems (POWER9, Little Endian).Kernel: RHEL 9 ships with kernel 5.14, which far exceeds Docker's minimum requirement of kernel 3.10.
